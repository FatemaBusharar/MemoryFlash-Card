Getting Started for Players
Open the Game

Launch the Memory Flash Card app on your device.

Choose a difficulty level (Easy, Medium, Hard).

Click Start to begin your memory challenge.

Flip the Cards

Tap or click on a card to flip it.

Flip a second card to try and find its matching pair.

Remember the card positions to improve your chances!

Match the Pairs

If the two cards match, they remain face up.

If they do not match, they flip back after a short delay.

Stay focused and keep track of previously revealed cards.

Watch the Timer & Score

Complete the level before time runs out.

Fewer moves = higher score.

Try to beat your best time!

Win the Game

Match all card pairs to win the round.

Your final score is displayed at the end.

Challenge yourself to replay and improve your performance!
