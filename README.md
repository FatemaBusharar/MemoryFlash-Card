### ***Description***
#### Boost your memory and sharpen your focus with this interactive flash card game! Flip cards to find matching pairs while racing against time. Each level increases the challenge, testing your concentration, speed, and recall abilities. Perfect for brain training, fun practice sessions, or competitive play with friends!

### ***Getting Started for players***

##### open the game
* Open the memory card game on your device.

* Choose your preferred difficulty level (Easy, Medium, or Hard).

* Press "Start" to begin the game.

* Flip two cards at a time to reveal their symbols.

* If the cards match, they remain face up. If they don't match, flip them over again.

* Try to remember the card positions to complete the board faster.

* Match all the pairs to win and achieve the highest score possible!
